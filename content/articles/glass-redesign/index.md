A major visual overhaul bringing a modern glass-forward aesthetic across the entire website.

**What's New:**

- **Glass-forward components** - Reimagined UI components with glassmorphism effects for a modern, layered look.
- **Refined homepage hero** - Updated hero layout with improved visual hierarchy.
- **Redesigned footer and navigation** - Cleaner, more cohesive footer and sidebar components.
- **Enhanced testimonial and partner sections** - Restyled testimonials slider and partner logos.
- **Improved content cards** - Article, video, and feature cards now feature the new glass aesthetic.

**Why This Matters:**

- **Modern visual identity** - A cohesive, contemporary design language throughout the site.
- **Better visual hierarchy** - Improved readability and content focus.
- **Consistent experience** - Unified styling across all pages and components.
