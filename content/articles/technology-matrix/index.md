An opinionated guide to cloud native and platform engineering technologies, visualized as a pipeline from discovery to advocacy.

**What's New:**

- **Pipeline visualization** - Technologies organized by adoption stage: Skip, Watch, Explore, Learn, Adopt, and Advocate.
- **Category lanes** - Browse by CNCF categories like Provisioning, Orchestration, Runtime, Observability, and more.
- **Advanced mode** - Interactive filtering, grouping, and exploration across all dimensions.
- **Technology cards** - Click any technology to see why I use it, spicy takes, and trajectory indicators.
- **Graveyard section** - A resting place for technologies that burned me, were abandoned, or have been superseded.

**Why This Matters:**

- **Opinionated guidance** - See where technologies sit in my personal evaluation and adoption journey.
- **Trajectory tracking** - Know which technologies are rising, stable, or falling in my estimation.
- **Newsletter integration** - Subscribe to get notified when technologies move through the matrix.
