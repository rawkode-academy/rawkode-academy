Express yourself with new color themes including popular developer favorites and Pride themes.

**What's New:**

- **Catppuccin themes** - Four flavors: Latte, Frappe, Macchiato, and Mocha.
- **Dracula theme** - The beloved dark theme for night owls.
- **Solarized themes** - Both light and dark variants of this classic.
- **Pride themes** - Rainbow and LGBTQ+ flag-inspired color schemes.
- **Theme submenu** - Access all themes directly from the command palette.

**Why This Matters:**

- **Personalization** - Match your coding environment with familiar themes.
- **Accessibility** - More options for different lighting conditions and preferences.
- **Expression** - Show your pride with inclusive theme options.
