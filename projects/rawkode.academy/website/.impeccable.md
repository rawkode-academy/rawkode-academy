## Design Context

_Working baseline captured on 2026-04-02 from the current site, brand system,
and homepage copy. Refine if the product direction changes._

### Users

Rawkode Academy is for cloud-native engineers, platform teams, and technical
leaders who need practical Kubernetes and infrastructure education. They arrive
to find trustworthy learning resources quickly, decide what to watch or read
next, and stay current without wading through generic marketing content.

### Brand Personality

Expert, candid, hands-on. The site should feel engineering-first, confident, and
human. It should avoid corporate polish that feels detached from real-world
building and instead communicate signal, momentum, and practical credibility.

### Aesthetic Direction

Light-first surfaces with subtle glass treatment, crisp typography, and
brand-led teal and green accents. The homepage should feel like a technical
editorial front page rather than a generic SaaS landing page. Real technology
marks, concrete content previews, and measured motion should carry more weight
than decorative effects. Avoid rounded card grids and boxed panel-heavy
compositions when a more fluid editorial layout can carry the content.

### Design Principles

- Lead with useful signal and obvious next actions.
- Keep the page cohesive by reusing one spacing, surface, and typography system.
- Prefer authentic proof of work over abstract claims.
- Use motion and gradients to support hierarchy, not as decoration for its own
  sake.
- Prefer open, flowing layouts with rules, whitespace, and typographic
  hierarchy over rounded containers and stacked cards.
- Preserve the academy's candid, builder-focused voice.
